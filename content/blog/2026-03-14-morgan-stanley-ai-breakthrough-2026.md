If these laws continue to hold—and there's no evidence yet that they've hit a wall—we're looking at models that could genuinely surprise us with their capabilities.

The "15-15-15" dynamic emerging—15-year data center leases at 15% yields, generating $15 per watt in value—suggests we're building infrastructure for a future where AI isn't a luxury but a necessity.

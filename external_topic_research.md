## Research Findings

### 1. Suno - Market Leader

**Statistics (February 2026):**
- 2 million paid subscribers
- $300M in annual recurring revenue (ARR)
- Rapid growth from subscriber base alone
- Major milestone: from industry pariah to major player

**Key Features:**
- Text-to-music generation
- High-quality audio output
- Web-based interface
- Subscription model

**Legal Journey:**
- Sued by RIAA and major record labels (2024)
- Warner Music Group settled in November 2025 for multi-million dollar deal
- Now partnering with major labels instead of fighting them
- From "angering the music industry" to "joining it"

### 2. Udio - The Competitor

**Key Features:**
- AI music generation platform
- Similar capabilities to Suno
- Also faced copyright lawsuits
- Settled with major labels
- No public API (different from Suno)

### 3. Industry Impact

**The Revolution:**
- AI music generation transformed the industry "overnight"
- From "Fake Drake" (2023) to label settlements (2025-2026)
- Major labels went from suing to partnering
- Revenue: $300M+ ARR for Suno alone

**Electronic Music Specifically:**
- DJ sets now incorporating AI-generated tracks
- AI remix tools for producers
- Real-time mix analysis and automation
- democratizing music production
- Debate about authenticity and human creativity

### 4. The Technology

**How It Works:**
- Neural networks trained on licensed music
- Text prompts generate original compositions
- Can specify genre, mood, instruments
- Extending to 4+ minute tracks
- Quality approaching human production

### 5. Key Timeline

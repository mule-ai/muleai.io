## Research Findings

### 1. AGI Predictions from Tech Leaders

**Elon Musk (xAI):**
- Predicts AGI (smarter than any single human) by end of 2026
- At Davos 2026: "We will have AI that is smarter than any one human probably by the end of this year"
- Says AGI will exceed collective human intelligence by 2027
- xAI's Grok models lead the charge, powered by 100,000+ Nvidia H100 GPUs in Memphis supercomputer

**Anthropic CEO Dario Amodei:**
- Predicts "powerful AI" by end of 2026
- Prefers term "powerful AI" over AGI (says AGI is overhyped)
- Focus on AI that could help with biology research, disease treatment

**OpenAI CEO Sam Altman:**
- Says "AGI kind of went whooshing by" already
- Now focused on "superintelligence" - AI that can do specific jobs better than any person (e.g., "CEO of a major company")

**Google DeepMind CEO Demis Hassabis:**
- More cautious - says we might wait another decade for true AGI
